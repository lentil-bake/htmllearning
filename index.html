Topics

Post Schedule

    Assorted writing, including...
  1. The Crater (every blue moon):
    a deep dive into a piece of media that has changed my mental landscape
  2. Withdrawal Symptoms (every month):
    a series reviewing books that have been withdrawn from collections elsewhere and deciding whether to keep them on my shelves
  3. Cover Story (every two weeks):
    a look at captivating, cultivated, and/or controversial book design and its effect on the text and the reader
  4. Etyms:
    shortposting about etymology
